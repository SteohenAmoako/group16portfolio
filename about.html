Dr. Stephen Moore

About Dr. Stephen

Stephen Edward Moore is a Computational Science and Engineering lecturer at the University of Cape Coast, trained in numerical analysis in Austria. He worked in several engineering companies and academic institutions in Austria before returning to Ghana to teach at the university.
